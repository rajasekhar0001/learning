SOLID Design principles:
Object Oriented Design plays a crucial role when it comes to writing flexible, scalable
maintainable and reusable code.

-> For good object oriented programming, having knowledge in SOLID principle is really need
SOLID principle is acronym for five principles

1) Single Responsibility Principle(SRP)
2) Open/Closed Principle
3) Liskov's Substitution Principle(LSP)
4) Interface Segregation Principle(ISP)
5) Dependency Inversion Principle (DIP)
